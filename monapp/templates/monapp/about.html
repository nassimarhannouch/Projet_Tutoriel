About Us

So why FeedbackFlow?

Our project FeedbackFlow is about creating an easy-to-use and smart online platform where students can give feedback on their courses, teachers, and learning experience. Instead of just collecting this feedback and leaving it unread, the platform will use artificial intelligence (AI) to automatically analyze the comments. The system will detect whether each message is positive, negative, or neutral, which helps turn raw opinions into useful insights.

The goal is to help teachers understand what students really think, so they can improve their teaching methods. For example, if many students say a course is too fast or confusing, the system will recommend ways to adjust the pace or clarify the lessons. These AI-powered suggestions will be sent directly to the teachers in a clear and friendly way.
